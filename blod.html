The importance of determining the blood group lies in blood donation operations, on which the blood groups that the patient can donate to or receive blood from are determined, as there are chemical structures called antigens on the surface of red blood cells, which determine the blood type, as there are two known systems that indicate the blood type, namely: ABO system: This represents blood type A, B, AB, or O, and depends on the presence or absence of antigen (A) and antigen (B) on the surface of red blood cells. Rh system: This represents the positive Rh factor
